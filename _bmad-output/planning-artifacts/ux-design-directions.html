Why it fits

  • Not necessary for one-step reserve; can be deferred to Phase 2.
  • If implemented, the hold must be explicit and time-boxed to avoid “phantom selection”.

Direction 6 — “Airy + admin/support pathway visible”

Employee UI stays calm, but the “arrival problem” pathway is deliberately surfaced.

A+C
Reserved: P07 Tue, 18 Mar
You’re set. Park in P07 today.
Reserved
Spot occupied? Open a support request with your reservation.
This direction encodes “arrival protection” into the UI so users feel supported.

Why it fits

  • Directly supports Step 4 emotional goals (not abandoned when issues happen).
  • Minimizes UI complexity: the pathway is present only when it matters (reserved state).
  • Works with both employee + support/admin journeys.
